Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan

My school

My school is called Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, Wayanad Kendra. There are
many Bharatiya Vidya Bhavans across India. It was founded on 7 November 1938 by K. M Munshi,
with the support of Mahatma Gandhi but the branch where I study was founded in 1993. My school
has an enrollment of about 1000 pupil and is one of the few CBSE curriculum schools which provide
higher secondary education in Wayanad district. It offers language courses in Malayalam, Hindi,
and even Sanskrit. Commerce, Biology and Computer streams are available for upper classes.


My school has all modern facilities like a library, Computer lab, Biology lab, Physics lab,
Chemistry lab and even an Audio Vision (AV) room. This year, the administration has even put
a Digital touchscreen board in one of the classrooms. My school has a large ground, perfect
for playing sports. The campus has a residential neighbourhood on one side and a forest on the
other side.
